Frequently asked questions

How do I log water?
Open the Today tab and tap the log button. Each tap adds water toward your daily goal and earns droplets toward your lifetime total.
What are tiers and droplets?
Every milliliter you log earns droplets that never reset. As your lifetime droplets grow you unlock new tiers on the Journey tab — from a shot glass all the way up to a dam.
How does the streak work?
Log at least once on a calendar day to keep your streak alive. Miss a day and a freeze token (you earn one every 7-day streak, up to 3) will protect it. Without a token, the streak resets.
Can I switch between ml and fl oz?
Yes. Droply supports both metric (ml/L) and imperial (fl oz/gal) units. Your preference is saved automatically.
Will I lose my progress?
Your progress is stored locally on your device. Lifetime droplets and tiers never reset on their own — only today's total resets each day. Because the data lives on your device, deleting the app removes it, but a full device backup (iCloud or Finder) restores it when you set up a new iPhone.
