Privacy Policy

Your data never leaves your phone.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Droply does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data — ever. Your privacy is simple to explain because there is nothing to explain.

The details

What we collect
Nothing. Droply has no account system, no analytics, no advertising, and no network requests of any kind.
Where your data lives
All of your data — lifetime droplets, daily logs, streak, freeze tokens, tier progress, daily goal, and unit preference — is stored only on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). It never leaves your phone and we never see it. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed by iOS.
Notifications
If you grant permission, Droply schedules local reminder notifications on your device to help you keep your hydration streak. These are generated entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to us or any third party. You can turn them off at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Droply.
Sharing
When you tap "Share" on a tier card, iOS presents the standard share sheet so you can send an image you created to apps of your choosing. Droply does not upload or transmit anything on its own — you decide where the image goes.
Third parties
Droply contains no third-party SDKs, trackers, or analytics. There are no external dependencies.
Children
Droply does not collect data from anyone, including children.
Changes
If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
